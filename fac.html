What is Faculty Aided Checkout?

Faculty Aided Checkout or FAC is a unique facility introduced by North South University for the first time in Bangladesh. In the Higher education field of Engineering and other Tech depended fields, often students are required to purchase various tools, equipments, softwares and other accessories. Which are often very expensive and burden on the student, also the process of purchasing such goods are not easy. One have to go through difficult legal paperworks just to avail international cards.

So, keeping all that in mind NSU have came forward to provide their students with easy checkout portal in which the student gets partial subsidies on the products, and if available by the vendor, also can get special benifits under student criteria.
